TASK 4 — SCENARIO WRITE-UP

In today’s digital environment, communication through email plays an important role in both academic and professional life. However, poor email etiquette can lead to misunderstandings and create a negative impression.

For example, a student might send an email to a professor requesting an assignment extension without a proper subject line, greeting, or polite tone. Such an email may appear careless or disrespectful, even if the request is genuine. As a result, the professor may ignore the email or not respond positively.

Similarly, when applying for internships, sending an unstructured or informal email without clear purpose or proper introduction can reduce the chances of getting selected. Companies expect professional communication, and a poorly written email may create a bad impression.

To avoid these issues, it is important to follow proper email etiquette. This includes using a clear subject line, starting with a respectful greeting, writing the message in a structured and polite manner, and ending with a proper closing.

Through this task, I understood that email communication is not just about sending a message, but about presenting oneself professionally. Following proper email etiquette can improve communication and create a positive impact in both academic and professional situations.
