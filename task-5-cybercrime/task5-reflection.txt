Task 5 Reflection – Cybercrime Awareness

Task 5 was one of the most eye-opening parts of this project for me. Before this, I had heard about cybercrimes like phishing, but I never really understood how they actually happen in real life. While working on this task, I realized that these attacks are not very complicated — they mostly depend on tricking people through simple methods like fake messages or links.

What surprised me the most was how real these fake websites and emails can look. It made me understand that anyone can become a victim if they are not careful. I also realized that small actions, like clicking on a link without checking, can lead to serious consequences such as losing personal data or money.

Creating the prevention checklist helped me think practically about how to stay safe online. It is not just about knowing the problem, but also about knowing how to prevent it. This task has made me more aware and cautious while using the internet. From now on, I will be more careful about what I click and what information I share online.
