#🔐 Cybercrime Prevention Checklist
#🛡️ Stay Safe Online
🔒 Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts
📱 Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for extra security
🚫 Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
🌐 Always check website URLs before entering personal details
🔑 Never share OTPs, passwords, or bank information with anyone
💻 Avoid using public or shared devices for sensitive logins
⚠️ Be careful of messages that create urgency or fear
✅ Use only trusted and official websites and apps
🔄 Keep your phone, apps, and system updated regularly
📥 Do not download files from unknown or untrusted sources
🚨 Report Cybercrime

🌐 Website: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in

📞 Helpline: 1930
