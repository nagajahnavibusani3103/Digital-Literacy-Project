Simple Ways to Stay Safe from Cybercrime
Always create strong passwords using a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for extra security
Never click on links from unknown or suspicious emails or messages
Carefully check website URLs before entering any personal information
Do not share OTPs, passwords, or bank details with anyone
Avoid logging into important accounts on public or shared devices
Be cautious of messages that create urgency or panic
Use only trusted and official apps or websites
Keep your phone and apps updated regularly
Think twice before downloading files from unknown sources
