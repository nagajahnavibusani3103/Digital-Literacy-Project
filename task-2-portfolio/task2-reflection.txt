Reflection on Digital Portfolio (Task 2)

While working on Task 2, I got the opportunity to create my digital portfolio using platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, HackerRank, and Kaggle. Initially, I did not fully understand the importance of maintaining an online professional presence, but this task helped me realize how useful these platforms can be for students.

Creating my GitHub profile and adding a README made me feel like I was taking my first step into the technical world. It gave me a sense of responsibility to start building and showcasing my work. Setting up my LinkedIn profile also made me aware of how important it is to present myself professionally, even as a first-year student.

Solving a problem on HackerRank was a small step, but it boosted my confidence in coding. Exploring Kaggle was also interesting, as it introduced me to datasets and the concept of data-driven learning.

One challenge I faced was understanding how these platforms are connected to real-world opportunities. However, after completing the task, I realized that they play a major role in building skills, networking, and preparing for internships.

Overall, this task helped me understand the importance of starting early, staying consistent, and building a strong digital identity. I plan to continue improving my profiles and using these platforms regularly in the future.
